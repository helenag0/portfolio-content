Seasons

L'Amour, the brand's Spring/Summer 2021 show, took place in a Parisian wheat field, with models striding down a 600-metre wooden runway that curled through the long grass. Replete with breezy dresses, gelato-hued separates and lace-up detailing, the entire collection immediately catapulted to the top of every fashion girl's wish list, and now it's finally landed in stores - just in time for our holiday social commitments. Designer Simon Porte Jacquemus celebrated the first drop from the SS21 collection has finally hit stores, posting a throwback to the now-iconic show on his Instagram. “BRING ME BACK,” he captioned the short video.

Miu Miu FW21

Collective strength, a community of spirit. Miuccia Prada draws together diverse groups - gangs of women - for the Miu Miu Fall/Winter 2021 fashion show. Coming together, the Miu Miu women set out on a journey through the mountains: exploring the landscape, on a quest heading towards an unknown goal. Bravery in endeavour is reflected by courage in clothing - they are dressed for the extreme. As a fashion show in the snow is incongruous, so garments can challenge perception and context. Outfits combine different intentions, different dreams and realities, to create different characters. Inside and outside, protective and seductive, - intentions are multiple, like the diverse identities found within each woman. And, in turn, each individual is attracted to liked-minded personas, the single becoming the collective. They form alliances, kinships. And they walk together.
